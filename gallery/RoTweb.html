ENGINE BLOCK ROTATOR

In this project, a motor block rotator was designed for control tasks between machining processes. It included dynamic calculations to select the command, calculations for sizing and selection of the pneumatic system for frame movement and block clamping, and FEM theoretical verification of the parts subjected to loads in said device.
